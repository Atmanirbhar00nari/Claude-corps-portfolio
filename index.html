Who I Am

A generalist who learns on purpose

"I don't just collect knowledge — I put it to work. Then I teach AI to do the same."

I've built my career across five very different industries — sports law media, dance education, healthcare, HR and exchange programs, and virtual assistance. Not because I couldn't choose, but because I believe every pivot adds a new lens.

What ties it all together: I've always been the person asking "how can we do this smarter?" Now that question leads me to AI. Not as a buzzword — as a daily practice. I use Claude to handle the monotonous so I can focus on the meaningful. I train AI assistants to need fewer prompts over time.

That's exactly what nonprofit organizations deserve: not a tech vendor, but a practitioner who makes AI actually work for their mission, their team, and the people they serve.

01 / Philosophy

Automate to Elevate

I use AI to eliminate the repetitive so my energy goes to research, strategy, and what genuinely requires a human being. The goal is always the bigger picture.

02 / Practice

Teach, Don't Just Use

I actively train my AI assistants to ask fewer questions over time. The goal is a system that knows you — not one you're constantly correcting. That's the difference between a tool and a collaborator.

03 / Mindset

Curiosity as OS

I'm not learning AI because it's trending. I've always been drawn to whatever helps me think, do, and give better. Right now, that's AI. Tomorrow it'll be whatever comes next.

What I'll Bring

My vision for nonprofit AI

Here's what I see when I imagine embedding inside a mission-driven organisation with Claude at the centre.

⚡

Eliminate the Mundane

Map every repetitive operational task — scheduling, data entry, reporting, email drafts — and replace them with AI-assisted workflows. Staff get their time back for actual mission work.

📖

Build Living Knowledge

Create AI-powered knowledge bases so institutional knowledge stops living in one person's head. When that person leaves, the system stays and the organisation keeps moving.

🌱

Teach, Not Just Do

Train staff to use AI themselves — not just hand them outputs. The goal is a team that's ten times more capable, not one that's dependent on a single AI person to function.

📡

Amplify the Mission's Voice

Help nonprofits communicate more powerfully — grant writing, impact reports, donor outreach — so the story of their work reaches everyone who needs to hear it.
